Player Movement
My group was indecisive about how we wanted the movement to be, so I offered to make two different versions so that they can see how the different types would be. I made one like Temple Run 2 were the player is not confined within lanes giving the player more freedoom. The second one I made was like Subway Surfers were the player is confined within lanes.

After the group saw them and discussed, we came to the conclusion that the lanes was a better fit for our game.

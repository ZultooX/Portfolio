About Me
Hello there! My name is Christopher Vedlund, and I'm a game nerd. I've had an interest in games and movies since as long as I can remember, my favorites are Star Wars and The last of Us.

I started learning coding back in 2020, at LBS High School, and I loved it. The satisfaction of seeing a character come to life and interact with the world is incredibly rewarding.
I enjoy doing smaller projects within my own game engine "Zengine", my goal is to create and release a game with it,

At TGA I feel that I really hugely improved my skills in a short period of time I really like but most of all I got to improve my gameplay development skills, by the 7 game projects so far.

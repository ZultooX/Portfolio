This was the first project that we made during the time studying at The Game Assembly. Made in Unity Game Engine in a timespan of 6 weeks, We used Race The Sun as our reference game to follow gameplay and make it similar.

Tree Editor

Making levels for a "infinite runner" and saving time can be hard. Making tools for the designers will decreases time on this process.

In short I designed a tool to place out trees that will fall in a sequence with many settings to tinker with, maybe too much for what it is used for.

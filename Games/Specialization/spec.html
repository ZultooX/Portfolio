Procedural Animations Using FABRIK
I chose to specialize in procedural animations using inverse kinematics. After some reading about different alghoritms i chose the FABRIK (Forward and Backwards Reaching Inverse Kinematics) to suit my needs the best, since it seemed the most optimized for games and easy to implement.

I also wanted to recreate the subtle hand animations from Uncharted 4 were the player reaches his hands towards walls as he walks close to them.
